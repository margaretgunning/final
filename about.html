DiMario's Wood Fired Specialty Pizza Restaurant & Take-Away is coming soon to you. Opening on 1st January 2021.

DiMarios was founded in 1980 by the Dimario. The DiMarios were born and raised in a little town called Alberobello in Itlay.

Their love for pizza comes from a long line of family and their devoted love to making the best pizzas in town. They have spent many years mastering their renowned and much loved secret sauce and their sought after pizza base. Word on the street is Dean Martin himself wrote the lyrics to his song 'Thats Amore' while visiting Alberobello in the DiMario family run business.

It is said that the one and only Dean Martin was in fact eating a pizza from DiMario's restaurant when he came up with the famous lyrics to this song, in particular the very well know lyrics "when the moon hits your eye like a big pizza pie, thats Amore".

DiMario's have a number of well established restaurants based in Itlay and the 'DiMario's Brothers' are branching out and bringing a little piece of Italy to Dublin. The three DiMario brothers, being Antonio, Piero & Francesco are bringing their much loved pizza franchise to Dublin 4 in 2021.
